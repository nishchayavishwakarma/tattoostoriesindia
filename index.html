Frequently Asked Questions

The cost of a tattoo depends on the size, style, and placement. Smaller tattoos may start at a few thousand rupees, while larger detailed pieces are priced higher. Contact us for a custom quote.

Pain varies depending on placement and individual tolerance. Most clients describe it as a scratching sensation that is very manageable.

Tattoos usually take 2–3 weeks to heal on the surface, but full healing can take up to 6 weeks. We’ll provide you with detailed aftercare instructions.
